Scope and interview

The process starts with two conversations: What your foundry needs and who your foundry is. We review your current solution, your history, business needs, experiences, and opportunities.

Write and sketch

Based on your interview, we write all the copy for your home, about, and any other evergreen pages. Once you review and approve the content, we sketch several key page designs for feedback and approval.

Design

With the direction established, we design and share full-fidelity mockups of key pages for review. After a round of feedback, we're ready to build the site.

Develop

Built on a vetted platform and simple Kirby CMS, we develop your new type foundry website, complete with analytics and search engine optimization.

Launch

Share the link, tell your friends, and cut the ribbon. Your site is live and ready to process font sales.

Grow

Add new fonts, publish case studies and fonts in use articles, sell your services, and build an audience.
